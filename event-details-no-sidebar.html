AGENCY OPEN HOUSE: RETAIL ADS THAT ROCK!

Join us for our monthly open house! In November we will review rockstar store-front advertising. Meet other retailers and get new ideas to bring customers into your store this holiday season. Networking will begin at 6:00pm and the presentation will begin at 6:30pm.

There will be time for question and answers so feel free to bring examples of your own advertising. Drinks and light snacks will be provided.

Paid parking is available in the building garage. There is street parking available around the building.
